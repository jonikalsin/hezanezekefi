Veronica Ruelas Job

Veronica goes around as an optometrist, entrepreneur, published author, influencer, and philanthropist.

She got her Bachelors in Psychology and Biology at Dominican College of Blauvelt in June 1995 and received her doctorate in Optometry from the New York School of Optometry in June 1998. She has since then been working as an optometrist among other jobs.

At the age of 46, when Veronica got married, she was an optometric physician in private practice in New York. Much before that in 2008, she had founded the non-profit ThirdEyeVision foundation. One could book an appointment at the clinic located at 220 Murray Street New York.

In January 2021, Veronica took on a new full-time role of founder and Co-CEO at FORGIVITY, a mobile app. And the next month, she co-founded Two Plume Productions.

Now with over 24 years of experience in successful optometric practice, she has been the driving force behind these behavioral and spiritual sciences.
