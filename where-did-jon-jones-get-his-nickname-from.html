Where Did Jon Jones Get His Nickname From?

May 20, 2024
blog

Jon Jones, a renowned figure in the world of Mixed Martial Arts (MMA), is widely known by his nickname "Bones."

This moniker has a deep-rooted history that dates back to his high school days and carries personal significance to the former UFC champion.

High School Football and the Birth of "Bones"

During his high school years, Jon Jones was an active participant in football, where his tall and slender physique earned him the enduring nickname "Bones."

As Jones himself recounts, his slim frame and towering height led his teammates to affectionately refer to him as "Bones," a name that resonated with his physical attributes on the football field.

This endearing nickname not only reflected his physique but also served as a nod to his brothers, Arthur and Chandler Jones, who pursued successful careers in the NFL.

Evolution of the Nickname

Interestingly, prior to adopting the moniker "Bones," Jon Jones had contemplated a different nickname for his professional MMA career.

In a somewhat lighthearted revelation, Jones disclosed that his initial fight nickname was intended to be "Sexual Chocolate."

But he humorously reflected on this choice, expressing disbelief at his own decision and indicating a departure from this choice.

Personal Reflections on the Nickname

Reflecting on the origins of his nickname, Jon Jones has shared personal anecdotes that provide further insight into the significance of "Bones."

Recounting his football days, he humorously described himself as a football player who "couldn't catch" and "couldn't throw," emphasizing his slender physique juxtaposed with large football gear.

His description of having "little chicken legs coming out of the uniform" captures the essence of how the nickname "Bones" encapsulated his physical attributes and distinguished him from his peers.

Legacy and Significance

Looking beyond the physical attributes that inspired his nickname, Jon Jones has articulated his aspirations to be remembered as a hard worker who defied odds and left an indelible mark on the sport of MMA.

Expressing a desire to be known for transcending the sport and inspiring others, Jones's reflection on his legacy adds depth to the significance of the nickname "Bones."

He embodies the spirit of resilience, determination, and a commitment to overcoming challenges.

The Chosen One?

While the nickname "Bones" has become synonymous with Jon Jones, alternative suggestions have emerged, such as "The Chosen One."

This proposed moniker aims to reflect Jones's impactful presence in the MMA realm, drawing upon his personal background and aspirations. But it is worth noting that this suggestion may not align with Jones's own preferences, as indicated by his brother, Arthur Jones.

On the whole

Jon Jones' nickname "Bones" holds a special place in his journey as a professional athlete.

Stemming from his high school football days, this moniker not only reflects his physical attributes but also embodies his resilience, determination, and ambition.

While alternative nicknames have been proposed, "Bones" remains deeply entrenched in the legacy of Jon Jones, symbolizing his unique journey and indelible impact on the world of MMA.

As Jon Jones continues to make his mark in the world of combat sports, the nickname "Bones" stands as a testament to his enduring legacy and the unique story behind its origin.
