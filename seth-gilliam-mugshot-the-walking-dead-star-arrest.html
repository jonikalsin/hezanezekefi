Seth Gilliam Mugshot: Why Was The Walking Dead Star Arrested?

Seth Gilliam was arrested in May 2015 in Peachtree City, Georgia, and booked for driving under the influence, speeding, and possessing marijuana.

At the time of the incident, Gilliam was in Georgia filming the sixth season of The Walking Dead.

The Police pulled over the Actor after he was clocked speeding at 107 mph in a 55 mph zone.

Upon further inspection, the Police found that Gilliam had been drinking and had a blood-alcohol content of 0.107, above Georgia’s legal limit.

According to the Police report, the arresting officer observed that Gilliam’s breath smelled strongly of alcohol, and his eyes were bloodshot and watery. The report also noted that Gilliam was unsteady.

Likewise, he had trouble keeping his balance during a field sobriety test. Gilliam admitted to the officer that he had consumed three beers and a shot of tequila before getting behind the wheel.

In addition to the DUI charge, Gilliam was charged with possession of marijuana after the Police found a small amount of the drug in his car. Gilliam later admitted to smoking marijuana earlier in the day.

However, he claimed that he had not consumed alcohol until after he had finished filming for the day. Gilliam was taken to the Fayette County Jail, where he was booked and had his mugshot taken.
