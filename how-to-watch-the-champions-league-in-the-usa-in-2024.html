How to watch the Champion's League in the USA in 2024

May 06, 2024
blog

The UEFA Champions League has become a global phenomenon and is eagerly followed by soccer fans in the USA.

This prestigious tournament has witnessed tactical advancements and emerging playing styles, making it a highly anticipated event in the soccer calendar.

Courtesy of Fan Arch, here's a comprehensive guide on how to watch the Champion's League in the USA in 2024.

Television Broadcast and Streaming Platforms

In the USA, there are several options to catch the action-packed Champion's League matches through television broadcast and streaming platforms:

Television Broadcast

  • CBS and CBS Sports Network: These channels have the exclusive English-language rights to broadcast UEFA Champions League games. This includes coverage from the Group Stage to the final, providing a comprehensive viewing experience for soccer enthusiasts. 

  • Univision, TUDN, and UniMas: These Spanish-language channels have acquired the rights to broadcast UEFA Champions League matches in Spanish, catering to a wider audience with diverse language preferences. 

Streaming Platforms

  • Paramount+: Paramount+ offers an exclusive streaming service for all Champion's League matches, with a 7-day free trial available. Additionally, some select matches are chosen for television broadcast on CBS or CBS Sports Network. 

  • Fubo: This streaming service provides an alternative option for viewers to watch Champion's League games, especially for the Spanish-language broadcast. 

  • ViX: NBC places select matches from the group stage on its ViX streaming platform, providing an additional streaming option for viewers. 

Annual Champions League Schedule

Understanding the annual schedule of the Champion's League can help fans stay updated with upcoming matches. The preliminary rounds begin in July, followed by the playoff rounds in August. The group stages of the competition are played from September through December. Subsequently, the knockout stages progress from February through to the final in May. 

  • Quarterfinals: April 9-10, 2024 (1st leg) | April 16-17, 2024 (2nd leg)
  • Semifinals: April 30-May 1, 2024 (1st leg) | May 7-8, 2024 (2nd leg)
  • Final: June 1, 2024 

Subscriptions and Pricing

For fans interested in subscribing to specific services for watching the Champion's League, there are various subscription options available:

  • Paramount+: Offers a 7-day free trial and provides access to live matches and extensive coverage of the Champion's League. 

  • Fubo: With a focus on Spanish-language broadcast, Fubo offers an alternative platform for streaming Champion's League games. 

  • ESPN+: Provides access to various soccer events including Bundesliga, LaLiga, Championship, and FA Cup, catering to a diverse range of soccer viewers. 

  • Peacock Premium: Offers exclusive access to 175 Premier League games per season, adding value to the soccer viewing experience.

Historical Broadcasting Evolution

The broadcasting landscape for the Champion's League in the USA has evolved over the years:

  • Late 1990s: The Champion's League started out on US TV on ESPN and gained prominence during the mid-2000s on ESPN. 

  • 2010: Broadcasts shifted to FOX Sports, where UCL games in English remained for nearly a decade. 

  • 2018: Coverage moved to Turner Sports, and English broadcasts on TNT and Bleacher Report Live were well received. 

  • 2021-2024: CBS secured English-language UEFA club competition rights, providing comprehensive coverage of games across Paramount+, CBS Sports Network, and the main CBS Network. 

The UEFA Champions League continues to captivate soccer fans in the USA with its exhilarating matches and compelling storylines.

The annual schedule, subscription options, and historical broadcasting evolution provide a comprehensive understanding of how to watch the Champion's League in the USA in 2024, ensuring an engaging viewing experience for soccer enthusiasts.
