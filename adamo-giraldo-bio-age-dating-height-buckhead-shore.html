Adamo Giraldo On Buckhead Shore

Joining the Jersey Shore franchise, MTV’s brand new reality series Buckhead Shore premiered June 23, 2022, and it saw 9 longtime friends chronicling “Lake Life” in Atlanta’s upscale Buckhead, Georgia. Yes, lake life! The “Shore” part of the title doesn’t actually refer to an ocean. It was just put there to let the viewers know that this was another show in the Jersey Shore franchise.

All excited for the viewers to see what the show has in store for them, Adamo took it to his IG to share, “tHeRs nO sHoRe iN bUcKheAd” @buckheadshore airs in 2 weeks on @mtv !!! @ 9 pm est…. Who am I seeing at our premiere party??”

Also, in an interview the star mentioned that being a part of the Shore family —  a franchise that helped MTV — was the “coolest thing ever.”

Joining Adamo for this inaugural season were his co-stars — JuJu Barney, Katie Canham, Savannah Gabriel, Parker Lipman, Bethania Locke, Pat Muresan, Chelsea Prescott, and DJ Simmons.
