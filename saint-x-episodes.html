Saint X: Hulus Latest True Crime Series Episodes and Release Schedule

July 16, 2024
blog

A brand-new psychological mystery series called Saint X has debuted on Hulu. The television adaptation of Alexis Schaitkin’s book of the same name follows a girl who is seeking explanations for her sister’s 1990s abduction and death. The story is spread across three separate time periods.

Audiences are enjoying the storyline and emotional connection of the series’ first episodes, which have earned great reviews. Hulu will instead release the show in weekly installments as opposed to releasing all of the episodes at once. So how many episodes of Saint X are there, and when do they air? Find out by reading on.

Saint X: Release Schedule

Eight episodes are scheduled for Saint X. As part of the series debut on April 26, the first three episodes were released concurrently. The remaining episodes will now show each Wednesday each week. The conclusion is slated for May 31. The whole release schedule is provided here:

Episode 1: A Lovely Nowhere: April 26th
Episode 2: Woman Is Fickle: April 26th
Episode 3: Men Of Interest: April 26th
Episode 4: A Disquieting Emptiness: May 3rd
Episode 5: Colonial Interference: May 10th
Episode 6: Loose Threads Of The Past: May 17th
Episode 7: The Goat Witch And The Sinner: May 24th
Episode 8: Faraway: May 31st
Every Wednesday at 12 a.m. PDT/3 a.m. ET, new episodes of shows are released on Hulu. To watch Saint X, you must have a current streaming platform subscription. A package sponsored by commercials from Hulu costs $7.99 per month or $79.99 per year. Cost of the ad-free plan each month is $14.99. A 30-day free trial is also available via the streaming provider.

What is Saint X About?

The show centres on a little child named Alison Thomas, who vanishes in the 1990s while on a family vacation in the Caribbean. Her sister, Claire, is still attempting to figure out what happened to Alison in the present. The inquiry is examined in a third timeline, which centres on the main suspect, Clive Richardson, whose life has been completely upended by the allegations.

“The series, which is told through multiple timelines, explores and upends the girl-gone-missing genre as it explores how a young woman’s mysterious death during a dreamy Caribbean vacation creates a traumatic ripple effect that eventually pulls her surviving sister into a dangerous pursuit of the truth,” according to the official synopsis.

Saint X is a Limited Series

There won’t be a second season of Saint X because Hulu has designated it as a limited series. But, if the first season performs remarkably well and there is a significant fan desire for the show’s renewal, things may change.

As Claire, West Duchovny plays Alison Thomas, Alycia Debnam-Carey plays Emily Thomas, Josh Bonzie plays Clive “Gogo” Richardson, Jayden Elijah plays Edwin, Michael Park plays Bill Thomas, Betsy Brandt plays Mia Thomas, and Bre Francis plays Sara in the show. Leila Gerstein is the show’s creator, and ABC Signature is in charge of production.
