The Meaning Behind The Song: “Always Something” by Inayah

TitleArtistAlbumRelease DateGenre
Always SomethingInayahInsecure (2022)May 13, 2022R&B

Have you ever listened to a song and felt like the lyrics spoke directly to your soul? “Always Something” by Inayah is one of those songs for me. Released in 2022 as part of her album “Insecure,” this R&B track explores themes of exhaustion, self-care, and the constant struggles we face in life.

The song begins with the lines, “I just need a little bit of self-care, I just wanna get the fuck up outta here.” These lyrics convey a sense of weariness and the desire for escape. We all reach a point in our lives where we need a break from the chaos and demands of the world. Inayah captures this sentiment perfectly, expressing the need for self-care and the longing to leave it all behind.

Throughout the song, Inayah emphasizes the idea that there is “always something” to do and “always something” to lose. This repetition highlights the relentlessness of life’s challenges. No matter how much we accomplish or how much we try to stay in control, there is always something that demands our attention or threatens to take away what we hold dear.

Personally, this song resonates with me on a deep level. As someone who tends to take on too much and has a hard time saying no, I often find myself overwhelmed and constantly chasing after the next task or goal. “Always Something” reminds me to prioritize my own well-being and take moments for self-care, even in the midst of a busy schedule.

The lyrics, “Tell my babies, ‘I ain’t leavin”” and “Tell my nigga, ‘I ain’t cheatin”” delve into the responsibilities and commitments we have in our personal relationships. Inayah acknowledges the importance of nurturing these connections while still honoring her own needs and desires. It serves as a reminder that we can find a balance between taking care of others and taking care of ourselves.

The bridge of the song repeats the phrase “it’s always something” multiple times, emphasizing the recurring nature of life’s challenges. Inayah’s vocal delivery captures the frustration and resignation that can come with facing these obstacles. However, the song’s outro provides a glimmer of hope, reminding us to find moments of self-care and escape even when it feels like there is “always something” to deal with.

Listening to “Always Something” by Inayah is like receiving a comforting embrace from a friend who understands the struggles you face. The honesty and vulnerability in Inayah’s lyrics make it easy to relate to her experiences, and it serves as a reminder that we are not alone in our battles.

For me, this song has become an anthem for those moments when life feels overwhelming and chaotic. It offers a reminder to prioritize self-care and find moments of solace in the midst of the chaos. “Always Something” has given me the strength to say no when I need to and take time for myself without guilt.

Ultimately, the meaning behind this song is a call to prioritize our own well-being and find moments of respite in the face of life’s challenges. It serves as a reminder that it’s okay to take a break, to say no, and to focus on ourselves when needed. Inayah’s powerful vocals and relatable lyrics make “Always Something” a song that resonates with listeners and encourages them to prioritize their own self-care and mental health.
