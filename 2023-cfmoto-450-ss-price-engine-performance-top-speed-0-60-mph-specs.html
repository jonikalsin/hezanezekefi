2023 CFMOTO 450 SS Price & Specifications

CFMOTO has launched a new middleweight sportbike for the US market, the 2023 CFMOTO 450 SS. With its affordable price tag and modern styling, this bike is expected to attract many sportbike enthusiasts. Let’s take a closer look at the design, features, specifications, safety, and price of the 2023 CFMOTO 450 SS.

Design and Look

The CFMOTO 450 SS features crisp, modern styling that is sure to turn heads. The bike’s designers aimed to make it look as fast as it feels by incorporating aerodynamic winglets on either side of the headlights. The bike is available in two colorways: Nebula White, which is primarily white with some black and electric blue accents, and Zircon Black, which has gray and red accents.

Features

Despite its affordable price, the CFMOTO 450 SS is loaded with features. The bike features full LED lighting all around, which not only looks great but also provides excellent visibility on the road. It also comes equipped with a 5-inch TFT display with Bluetooth connectivity. To connect, riders need to download the CFMOTO Ride App on their smartphone of choice.

Specifications

The 2023 CFMOTO 450 SS is has a 450cc, dual overhead cam, liquid-cooled parallel twin engine with bore and stroke of 77mm x 55.2mm. It produces a claimed 50 horsepower at 9,500 rpm and 28.8 pound-feet of torque at 7,600 rpm. The bike comes with a six-speed gearbox and a slipper clutch. The suspension make up of an upside-down fork up front and a multi-link suspension in the rear, with no adjustability mentioned at either end. The bike rolls on a pair of 17-inch alloy wheels.

Safety

Safety is a critical concern for any rider, and the CFMOTO 450 SS doesn’t skimp in this area. The bike comes with a single Brembo M40 radially mounted, four-piston caliper up front with a 320mm disc and a floating single-piston caliper in the rear with a 220mm disc. Continental ABS comes standard, providing riders with the confidence they need to ride with peace of mind. However, it is important to note that riders must wear appropriate gear, including helmets, gloves, and leather, to ensure their safety while riding.

Price

One of the most attractive features of the CFMOTO 450 SS is its affordable price tag. At just $5,499 in the US market, this bike provides an excellent value for riders looking for a middleweight sportbike without breaking the bank.

Conclusion

Overall, the 2023 CFMOTO 450 SS is an excellent option for riders looking for an affordable middleweight sportbike. With its sleek and modern styling, loaded features, and excellent value, this bike is sure to be a hit with riders looking to upgrade or enter the sportbike market. With the added safety features, riders can feel confident and secure while enjoying their ride.
